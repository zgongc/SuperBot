All conditions work with an AND logic: All conditions you specify for generating the input signal must be met.

Example:

  • RSI-14 < 35 VE
  • EMA-9 crossover EMA-21 VE
  • Volume > Volume-SMA

These 3 conditions at the same time when a LONG input signal is generated.
